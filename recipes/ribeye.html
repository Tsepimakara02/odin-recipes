RECIPE: Boneless Grilled Ribeye Steak with Herb Combound Butter

by Tyus Makara

"The Perfect Ribeye"

What You'll Need

For the Steak

  • 16-24oz Prime Ribeye
  • Salt
  • Black Peppercorns

For the Herb Compound Butter

  • 1 Stick of Salted butter
  • 1/4 cup of Fresh Cilantro finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup Fresh Parsley finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup Fresh rosemary finely chopped
  • 1 Tsp of MSG
  • Sheet of Plastic Wrap

Instructions

For the Steak

PREPARATION: Between 1-6 hours before grilling, Dry Brine the steak by seasoning both sides generously with salt (Kosher is king) and resting it on a wire rack until dry and a dark red color. Add peppercorns right before cooking. Ready the grill.

COOKING: With the grill scorching, lay the steak on and press it down. Flip after 1 minute then repeat until the external is a nice dark brown color--Usually 4 flips with a minute on each side.

Move the ribeye to the cool side of the grill and put the lid on. Let the steak rest on each side for 1 minute like this afterwhich remove the steak resting it again for 6+ minutes on a wire rack (be sure to clean the wire rack if reusing the same one from the dry brine process). You should have a bomb steak at this point.

For the Herb Compound Butter

PREPARATION: Get the stick of butter down to room temperature, unwrap (optional), and toss in a mixing bowl. Add your cilantro, parsley, rosemary, and pepperflakes and stir until mixed. Add salt if wanted. Lay down a sheet of plastic wrap and place contents in the middle, then tie the ends tightly and roll until a cylinderical shape takes form. Refridgerate until firm. Slice and place a piece on the steak while hot.
